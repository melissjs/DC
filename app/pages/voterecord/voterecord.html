Democracy Counts Thank you for participating in the Democracy Counts! 2016 General Election Audit. There are three parts to fill out and this process takes about three minutes. The three parts to fill out are:

1) An affidavit
2) Your vote and voter preferences
3) Demographic information

The objective of this independent, non-profit and volnteer organized audit is to safegaurd election inegrity by providing data that will be compared with the official recorded vote share at this polling station. If this audit uncovers substantial vote share discrepensies, our data will be instramental in potential legal proceedings.

*Your personal information is kept private and in a completely separate database than your vote. Your vote will never be linked to your personal information.
